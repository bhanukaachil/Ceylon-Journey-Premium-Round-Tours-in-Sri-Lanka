# Ceylon Journey – Premium Round Tours in Sri Lanka 🌴✈️

Welcome to **Ceylon Journey**, your premier travel partner for exploring the breathtaking beauty, rich heritage, and vibrant culture of Sri Lanka. This platform is designed to provide travelers with a seamless, premium experience for planning personalized round tours across the "Pearl of Asia."

---

## 🌟 Project Overview

Ceylon Journey is a high-end web application that showcases the diverse attractions of Sri Lanka. From the misty mountains of the Hill Country to the golden sands of the southern coast, the platform helps users discover and plan their perfect island getaway.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **Personalized Itineraries**: Tailor your travel plans based on interests, budget, and travel style.
- **Curated Tour Packages**: 
  - **Short Stays (< 5 Days)**: Cultural and historical highlights (Sigiriya, Kandy, Negombo).
  - **Popular Choice (8–10 Days)**: Comprehensive island circuit including Nuwara Eliya and Yala.
  - **Grand Exploration (10–14 Days)**: Deep dive into the North (Jaffna), East (Trincomalee), and South.
- **Destination Guides**: Detailed overviews of Sri Lankan beaches, wildlife, history, adventure, and cuisine.
- **Interactive Experience**: Smooth animations, premium hero banners, and a responsive design for all devices.
- **Direct Engagement**: Integrated contact forms and "Create Your Own Tour" functionality.

---

## 🛠️ Technology Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+).
- **Frameworks**: [Bootstrap 5](https://getbootstrap.com/) for responsive layout and UI components.
- **Typography & Icons**: 
  - [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) (Playfair Display, Poppins).
  - [Font Awesome 6](https://fontawesome.com/) for premium iconography.
- **Assets**: Optimized images (including `.avif` format) and custom CSS modules.
